Frequently Asked Questions

How does same day delivery work?
Orders placed before 4 PM (within 5 hours of working hours) are delivered the same day. Orders after 4 PM are delivered next day by 9 AM.
Do you deliver nationwide?
Yes! We deliver across India through our network of trusted florists. Mumbai and surrounding areas get special same-day delivery.
Can I include a custom message?
Absolutely! You can choose from our predefined messages or write your own personal message for the recipient.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe), and Cash on Delivery.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive an SMS with tracking details. You can also check order status in "My Account".
What if recipient is not home?
Our delivery partner will call before delivery. If unavailable, they'll attempt delivery again or leave with a neighbor as per your instructions.
