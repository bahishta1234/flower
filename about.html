The Language of Flowers

For centuries, flowers have been used to convey messages and emotions without uttering a single word. This beautiful tradition, known as floriography, allows us to express feelings that words sometimes cannot capture.

Each flower carries its own meaning and symbolism:

  • Roses - Love, passion, and romance
  • Lilies - Purity, virtue, and refined beauty
  • Sunflowers - Adoration, loyalty, and happiness
  • Orchids - Luxury, strength, and exotic beauty
  • Tulips - Perfect love and elegance
